# Eunsun Kim

I am a PhD candidate in Quantitative Marketing at the UCLA Anderson School of Management.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Quantitative Marketing and Industrial Organization, with a focus on algorithmic pricing and competition in digital markets. I combine economic theory, high-frequency cross-platform pricing data, and reinforcement learning methods to study how artificial intelligence interacts with market power.
